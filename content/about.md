# About Paper

Paper is a minimal blogging platform built with [Nuxt](https://nuxt.com) and the [Nuxt Content](https://content.nuxt.com) module. It is designed to feel like reading a well-typeset journal — clean, fast, and focused entirely on the words.

## The Philosophy

We believe the best reading experiences are the ones that get out of the way. No dark patterns. No engagement metrics. Just content, beautifully presented.

## Technical Stack

- **Framework:** Nuxt 4 with SSG
- **Styling:** Pure CSS, no frameworks
- **Typography:** Crimson Pro & Inter
- **Hosting:** Ready for Cloudflare Pages
